### C3 complete — Shared planning model (derived summary layer)

Migration 030 confirmed run. Picked up directly at C3.

**`GET /api/global-context/summary`** — new derived endpoint, no schema changes.

Pull model: the summary endpoint queries across `global_commitments`, `projects`, `quarterly_budget`, and `properties` in parallel and computes the derived picture server-side. No push/sync mechanism needed at this stage.

**Financial rollup:**
- `global_commitments` normalized to annual equivalent per recurrence type (annual × 1, monthly × 12, quarterly × 4, one_time in current year × 1); broken down by domain (parcel / personal / unassigned)
- Parcel: total `target_budget` across active/planned/on_hold projects; total `actual_spend` across all projects; sum of `core_expenses + additional_expenses` from `quarterly_budget` for current year

**Upcoming list:**
- Active/planned/on_hold projects with `target_year = current year` and `target_quarter` = current or next quarter (plus unscheduled current-year projects)
- One-time and quarterly commitments due in current or next quarter
- Annual/monthly commitments intentionally excluded — they belong in the financial rollup, not the action list
- Sorted by year, then quarter (null last)

**Conflict signals:**
- Over-budget projects (actual_spend > target_budget) → `warning`
- Threshold violations from `global_context.thresholds` JSONB against known keys (`parcel_annual_budget`, `parcel_annual_spend`, `annual_commitments`) → `warning` at 85%, `critical` at 100%

**Query pattern:**
1. Get lattice_id
2. Parallel: global_context (thresholds), global_commitments, property_members (owned)
3. Short-circuit if no owned properties (returns commitments data, empty Parcel data)
4. Parallel: projects, quarterly_budget for current year, properties (for names)
5. Compute all derived values in TypeScript

TypeScript clean, build clean.
